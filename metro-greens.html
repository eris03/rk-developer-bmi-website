Live Where the Forest Begins

Metro Greens is a monumental 200-acres ecological paradise designed for those who seek a life beyond the city's concrete walls. This isn't just a plot — it's an investment in a lifestyle that balances luxury with the serenity of the earth.

We are currently offering Phase 1, spanning 11.5 acres of premium, high-appreciation land near key development hubs. With a "Forest-First" approach, thousands of trees, organic gardens, and scenic walking trails define the Metro Greens horizon.
