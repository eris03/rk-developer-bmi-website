Project Overview

Where Business Flourishes

RK Revenue is Bangalore's next landmark commercial destination — a meticulously planned mixed-use development in Hebbal, one of the city's fastest-growing corporate and IT hubs. Designed for the discerning investor and entrepreneur, it brings together premium office suites, high-street retail, and food & beverage spaces under one iconic roof.

Spread across 2.8 acres with a 20-storey commercial tower, RK Revenue offers 180 units with flexible configurations — from compact starter offices to sprawling corporate floor plates — all within minutes of Bangalore International Airport and key IT parks.
