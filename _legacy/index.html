Introduction

A Legacy of Trusted Living Spaces

Bengaluru Metro City Infrastructure Housing Co-Operative Society Ltd. (BMI) is one of Karnataka's most respected housing societies — committed to creating sustainable, premium, and community-driven residential layouts.

We believe a home is more than four walls — it's a foundation for the future. Every BMI layout is built on three pillars: transparency, legality, and long-term value.

  • RERA & DTCP Approved Layouts
  • Clear Titles & Bank Loan Ready
  • Premium Amenities & Gated Security
  • Strategic Locations near Metro & IT corridors
