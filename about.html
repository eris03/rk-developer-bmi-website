Who We Are

Built on Trust,
Driven by Vision

RK Developers was founded with one unwavering goal — to make quality land and residential investments accessible, transparent, and rewarding for every buyer. Since our founding, we have successfully delivered 20+ projects across Bengaluru's most strategic growth corridors.

From residential layouts and farm plot communities to commercial developments, every RK project is backed by clear legal titles, thoughtful planning, and a commitment to on-time delivery. We serve buyers who value trust as much as returns.

Our track record spans Bagalur, Hesaraghatta, Yelahanka, Nelamangala, and Devanahalli — locations chosen for their high-appreciation potential and connectivity to Bengaluru's expanding infrastructure.

Our Vision

To be Bengaluru's most trusted land and residential developer — delivering value that appreciates with every passing year.

Our Mission

To offer legally clear, well-planned, and fully developed properties that empower families and investors to build a secure future.
